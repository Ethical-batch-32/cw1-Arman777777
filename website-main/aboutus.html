ABOUT US

Samsung is dedicated to following local laws and regulations while also enforcing a strong worldwide code of conduct for all workers. It believes that ethical management is a vehicle for developing confidence with its different stakeholders, including customers, shareholders, workers, business partners, and local communities, as well as a tool for adapting to rapid changes in the global business environment. Samsung continues to teach its personnel and operate monitoring systems, all while implementing fair and transparent corporate management, in order to become one of the most ethical organizations in the world.

Mission

Samsung has a simple corporate philosophy: to commit its expertise and technology to developing exceptional goods and services that improve the world. Samsung places a high importance on its people and technology in order to achieve this.

The values that define Samsung's spirit

Samsung believes that successful business is built on solid principles. This is why, in addition to a strict code of conduct, these basic principles are at the center of every choice the organization makes.

Five Samsung business principles

Samsung Electronics established the "Five Samsung Business Principles" in 2005 as a statement of its commitment to corporate social responsibility as a global leader. The principles serve as the cornerstone for the company's worldwide code of conduct, which ensures that it follows legal and ethical guidelines while also fulfilling its corporate social duties.
